Mango Cookies

Total Time: 1 hour Yield: 24 Cookies

  • Ingredients:
  • 3 eggs
  • 2 cups sugar
  • 2 packages Fruitastical Mango
  • 2 grams chocolate
  • 1 cup cream

Method

  • First and foremost you will need to open your Fruitastical mango pulp and thaw
  • Secondly, once thawed, add the mangoes to the cream and bring to a simmer
  • Take all the sugar and add to the egg whites. Save the egg yolks and whisk into the cream after removing it from the stove
  • Whip egg whites and sugar until stiff peaks form
  • Add chocolate to mango custard
  • Spoon out maringue onto a baking sheet and bake
  • Assemble cookies and enjoy
